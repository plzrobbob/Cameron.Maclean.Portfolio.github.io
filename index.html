Breath of The Coyote Unity Project

Hello! I am a young programmer currently working on several side projects in both Unity and Unreal Engine 4. For this project, I created all of the visual effects shown and helped design the level, with my direct contributions on the level design being helping with the initial layout of the map, the creation of the clouds in the game and creating a layout for the different puzzles and tasks in the level. In addition, I worked on quite a bit of the code, including some of the movement and damage scripts, all of the visual effects scripts, and some of the sound in the game.
